Tickets on Sale!

Basketball season is nearly upon us, which means it's almost time to cheer on your Charming Crows! This year promises to be their toughest season in recent memory, but we're confident that our state champion fightin' birds will keep the title in Charming for a second year running!

Our trusty mascot Sam the Crow will be causing a ruckus at each game, along with the Charming Cheerleaders, but we need your support. Tickets are on sale now, with the usual discount for our faithful Crow Boosters.
